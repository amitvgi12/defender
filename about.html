Our Story

Who We Are

Defender Security & Manpower Services was founded in Dehradun with a singular mission: to provide dependable, professional security services to the people and businesses of Uttarakhand.

Over the years, we have grown from a small local operation to one of the most trusted names in security across the region, deploying hundreds of trained guards and manpower professionals to clients ranging from government institutions and hospitals to hotels and industrial facilities.

We are PSARA licensed and operate with unwavering commitment to professionalism, integrity, and the safety of every client we serve.

Our Journey

Company Milestones

2012

Founded in Dehradun

Defender Security Services was established near Kargi Chowk, Dehradun with a small team and a big vision for professional security in Uttarakhand.

2015

PSARA Licensing Obtained

Officially licensed under the Private Security Agencies Regulation Act, cementing our commitment to legal and professional operations.

2017

Expanded to Manpower Services

Launched our professional manpower and staffing division to serve the growing hospitality and facility management sectors in Uttarakhand.

2019

50+ Corporate Clients Milestone

Reached a landmark of over 50 corporate clients including government bodies, hospitals, educational institutions, and commercial facilities.

2022

500+ Guards Deployed

Grew our workforce to over 500 trained and certified security professionals, making us one of the largest security agencies in Uttarakhand.

2025

Continued Growth & Digital Presence

Launched enhanced digital platforms to better serve clients and streamline operations, while continuing to expand our service coverage across the state.
