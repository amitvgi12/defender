Our Story

Who We Are

Defender Security & Manpower Services was founded in Dehradun with a singular mission: to provide dependable, professional security services to the people and businesses of Uttarakhand.

Since 2022, we have grown from a small local operation into one of the most trusted security names in the region, deploying trained guards and manpower professionals to clients including government institutions, hospitals, hotels, and industrial facilities.

We are PSARA licensed and operate with unwavering commitment to professionalism, integrity, and the safety of every client we serve.

Our Journey

Company Milestones

  1. 2022

    Founded in Dehradun

    Defender Security Services was established near Kargi Chowk, Dehradun with a small team and a clear vision for professional security in Uttarakhand.

  2. 2023

    PSARA Licensing Obtained

    Officially licensed under the Private Security Agencies Regulation Act, cementing our commitment to legal and professional operations.

  3. 2023

    Expanded to Manpower Services

    Launched our professional manpower and staffing division to serve the growing hospitality and facility management sectors in Uttarakhand.

  4. 2024

    20+ Corporate Clients Milestone

    Reached a landmark of over 20 corporate clients including government bodies, hospitals, educational institutions, and commercial facilities.

  5. 2025

    100+ Guards Deployed

    Grew our workforce to over 100 trained and certified security professionals, making us one of the largest security agencies in Uttarakhand.

  6. 2025

    Digital Expansion

    Launched enhanced digital platforms to better serve clients and streamline operations, continuing to expand service coverage across the state.
